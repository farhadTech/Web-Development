HTML headings are used to define the hierarchy (levels) and structure of content on a web page. They create a visual hierarchy, with the highest level heading which is h1 indication the most important content or the main heading, and lower-level headings like h2, h3, h4, etc. for subtopics. Reason to use Headings: Headings are crucial for struturing content and providing a clear visual indication of the beginning of new sections or topics. Properly structured headings readability and user experience on websites, ensuring that information is organized and easy to navigate.
